Privacy Policy

DNU Student Council TikTok Analytics Tool

Last Updated: May 06, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how the Students' Council of Dnipro National University named after Oles Honchar ("DNU Student Council," "we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the DNU Student Council TikTok Analytics tool (the "Service"). This Service is a private tool designed for internal analytics of the @dnu.official TikTok account.

1. Information We Collect

The Service is designed to collect specific, limited information from the @dnu.official TikTok account via the official TikTok API, only after explicit authorization is granted by an authorized representative of the DNU Student Council through TikTok's OAuth 2.0 process. The information collected includes:

  • Video Performance Data: For each video on the @dnu.official account, we may collect metrics such as view count, like count, comment count, share count.
  • Video Metadata: This includes information such as video ID, video duration, video creation/upload date, and share URL, as provided by the TikTok API.
  • Account Information (Limited): We may access basic public profile information of the @dnu.official account necessary to identify the account and retrieve its video list, such as the account's Open ID or User ID provided by the TikTok API.

We do not collect personal data from viewers of the @dnu.official TikTok account or any other TikTok users. We do not collect sensitive personal information.

2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used exclusively for the following internal purposes by the DNU Student Council:

  • To analyze the engagement and performance of video content posted on the @dnu.official TikTok account.
  • To generate internal reports to understand content effectiveness.
  • To inform and improve the content strategy for the @dnu.official TikTok account, aiming to better serve the DNU student community.
  • To populate a private Google Sheet with the collected metrics for easy access and analysis by authorized council members.

3. How We Share Information

The information collected through the Service is not shared with any third parties.

Access to the collected data (stored in a Google Sheet) is strictly limited to authorized members of the DNU Student Council who require it for their analytical tasks.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • The collected data is stored in a Google Sheet within the DNU Student Council's private Google Workspace environment.
  • We take reasonable measures to protect the information stored, leveraging the security features provided by Google Workspace.
  • Access to the Google Sheet is controlled through Google Workspace permissions.

5. Data Retention

We retain the collected video performance data as long as it is deemed useful for analytical purposes by the DNU Student Council or as required to comply with any operational needs. Data can be manually deleted from the Google Sheet by authorized council members.

6. Your Rights (as the @dnu.official Account Owner)

As the owner of the @dnu.official TikTok account, you have the right to:

  • Revoke the Service's access to your TikTok account data at any time through your TikTok account settings.
  • Request the deletion of data collected by the Service by contacting the DNU Student Council.

7. Children's Privacy

This Service is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13 (or the relevant age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data related to children. The @dnu.official account is managed by university students for a university audience.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify authorized users of any significant changes. Your continued use of the Service after any modification to this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such modification.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data handling practices, please contact the DNU Student Council at ttucbkotp9ic@gmail.com.
