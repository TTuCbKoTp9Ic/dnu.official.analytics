DNU Student Council TikTok Analytics Tool

About This Tool

The DNU Student Council TikTok Analytics Tool is a custom-developed, private utility designed exclusively for the Students' Council of Dnipro National University named after Oles Honchar. Its sole purpose is to assist the council in analyzing the performance of their official TikTok account, @dnu.official.

How It Works

This tool utilizes the official TikTok API to securely fetch publicly available performance metrics from the @dnu.official TikTok account. This requires a one-time authorization by an administrator of the @dnu.official TikTok account.

The data collected includes:

  • Video Views
  • Video Likes
  • Video Comments
  • Video Shares
  • Video Upload Date
  • Video Duration

This information is then organized and displayed within a private Google Sheet, accessible only to authorized members of the DNU Student Council.

Purpose and Usage

The primary goal of this tool is to provide the DNU Student Council with data-driven insights into their TikTok content. This allows them to:

  • Understand what type of content resonates best with the DNU student community.
  • Track engagement trends over time.
  • Improve their content strategy to better serve and inform Dnipro National University students.

Privacy and Security

  • This tool is for internal use only by the DNU Student Council.
  • Data is fetched directly from the TikTok API after explicit user consent.
  • The collected data is stored securely within the DNU Student Council's private Google Workspace environment.
  • No data is shared with any third parties.
  • We are committed to handling data responsibly and in accordance with our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
